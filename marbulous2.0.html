Our Mission

The problem
is real.

Up to 90% of menstruating individuals experience painful periods. This isn't a niche issue — up to 40% of students and 45% of professionals have missed school or work due to menstrual pain.

Current solutions are bulky and restrictive — they push people to stay home and pause their lives. Disposable heat patches are wasteful. Painkillers carry systemic side effects.

Marbulous changes that. We've created high-rise compressive period underwear with a hybrid treatment system — turning everyday wear into a continuous, medical-grade pain-relief solution. A modular build lets users add the heat and vibration module in and out. Reusable, sustainable, life-changing. Nothing like this exists.

How It Works

Dual-pathway
neuromodulation.

🌡️

Thermal Treatment

Carbon fiber tape delivers uniform thermal flux, inducing vasodilation in myometrial tissue. Increased blood flow accelerates clearance of prostaglandins — the primary chemical trigger for uterine contractions.

Somatic Relief
〰️

Vibrotactile Modulation

Multiple LRAs spaced 4–6 cm apart stimulate cutaneous mechanoreceptors. Based on Gate Control Theory, faster mechanical signals saturate the neural pathway — blocking visceral pain transmission to the brain.

Neural Perception
💧

High-Capacity Absorption

Superabsorbent polymer hydrogel sheets with 3× the capacity of a standard pad. The modular build integrates seamlessly with the treatment system — comfort and care in one garment.

Protection
🔬

Smart Diagnostics (Future)

Embedded lateral flow test strips will estimate cholesterol, pH for infection detection, and protein markers for inflammation and anemia — delivering real-time non-invasive health insights.

Biosensing
📡

Connected Platform (Future)

An integrated ESP32 microcontroller will sync all health data to a cell phone, transforming everyday menstrual care into a smart, connected health monitoring platform.

IoT Health

Reusable and built to last. At $89–99 retail with a 40% gross margin, the product reaches payback in under 6 months — delivering a 745% ROI versus recurring disposable solutions.
